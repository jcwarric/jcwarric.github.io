Midland Area Catholic Community


Welcome!

The four parishes of the Midland Area Catholic Community are partnering to enrich parish life for all local Catholics. Working in collaboration, we can enable greater opportunity for worship, stronger faith formation, ongoing study and rich sacramental experiences no matter which parish we call home. Through this process, our hope is that each individual will experience in greater abundance the fullness and grace of God, and a future full of hope as we move together into greater Community.
