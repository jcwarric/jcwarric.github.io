Partnership Advocacy Team

History

Better decription to be added- The quick background story is that the MACC Partnership Advocacy Team (PAT) had formed a few task groups to facilitate bringing Catholics in the area (Midland and Sanford) together over the past several years beginning when the Diocese orchestrated the formation of "cohorts" to determine the best re-configuring groups of parishes to address a variety of issues that had occurred. In the MACC group of four parishes, we formed a Partnership of parishes as we continued to have sufficient income and priests to not only continue operating as individual parishes but also to look ahead into the future to a time when that might not be the case.
